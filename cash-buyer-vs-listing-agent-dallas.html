Cash Buyer vs. Listing with an Agent in Dallas: Real Numbers

Most sellers fixate on the sale price. But that's only part of the equation. Here's what you actually walk away with — in both scenarios.

Let's say your Dallas home has an estimated market value of $280,000. You have two main options: list with an agent and wait for a buyer, or sell directly to a cash home buyer. Here's how both scenarios actually play out.

Gross Sale Price$280,000

The Real Difference: $9,100

In this example, the cash offer nets you only ~$9,100 less than the traditional listing — and you get it in 7 days instead of 75+, with none of the hassle, repairs, or uncertainty. For many sellers, that trade-off is a clear win.

When Does Each Option Make More Sense?

List with an agent if: Your home is in great condition, you're not in a hurry, and maximizing the gross sale price is your top priority.

Sell to a cash buyer if: You need to move quickly, your home needs significant repairs, you're in a financial bind, or you simply want certainty and zero hassle.
